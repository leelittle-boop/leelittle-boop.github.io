The Founder's Story

Building the financial infrastructure the next great companies deserve.

Financial strategy is only as powerful as the execution engine behind it. To command authority in high-complexity sectors, I intentionally built a background that bridges the gap between human leadership, strict regulatory compliance, and technological precision.

Before founding SL Accounting Solutions CPA, I spent years navigating the high-stakes world of Financial Operations and Risk Management for global leaders. I saw firsthand how the right financial architecture could protect a company from risk while fueling massive innovation.

I realized that while large corporations have entire departments dedicated to their financial health, many startups and small businesses are left to navigate the complexities of scale alone. I founded this firm to change that — to give founders the same high-level expertise and strategic leadership I brought to corporate boardrooms, but with the agility and personal touch of a boutique firm.
